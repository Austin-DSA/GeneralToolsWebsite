What this is checking

  • Sign-ons - proponent + 35 members for a bylaws amendment (Section 10.1); 25 for a project committee or campaign (Section 7.1). General resolutions and candidate endorsements need none, so they show no threshold.
  • Stage - "Gathering sign-ons" collects toward the threshold; "On the agenda" means you have scheduled it for a meeting and closed sign-ons. Open one and use Record the vote to adopt or reject it.
  • Where it lives - members read and sign the text in Echo while it gathers; once adopted the text is frozen, kept here as the record, and exported to the chapter's resolutions repo.
  • Vote threshold - bylaws amendments and candidate endorsements need a two-thirds vote; everything else a simple majority of those voting.
